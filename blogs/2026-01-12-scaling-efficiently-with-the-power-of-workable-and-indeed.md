City Wide Facility Solutions is a third-generation, family-owned building services management company founded in 1961. Operating across 110 locations nationwide and in Canada, the organization manages everything from janitorial services to landscaping, maintenance, and specialty facility operations through a unique B2B model that partners with independent contractors. Hiring for City Wide spans a wide range of roles, from night managers to sales executives and even C-suite leadership.
