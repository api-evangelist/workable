While other HR tech companies typically allocate 15–25% of their revenue to R&D, Workable invests 35%, significantly above the industry average. This commitment has helped keep Workable ahead of the curve for years, particularly in intelligent automation and AI. Long before the AI boom, over six years ago, Workable established a dedicated team specialized in the development of AI, refining the art of blending machine intelligence with human insight, and it’s paying off.
