A major shift is underway in hiring, and we’re seeing it across Workable every day. Around 10% of the job applications flowing through our platform are now submitted via AI mass-apply tools. But what does this signal for employers? Is it a genuine disruption, or simply a misunderstood change in candidate behavior? To unpack this, Workable CEO Nikos Moraitakis offers important insight: “These aren’t ‘fake’ candidates. The vast majority are real people with real resumes.” For some recruiters, the surge in AI-assisted applications feels like pollution. The instinct is to block them entirely.
