Who is We Are Social? We Are Social is a global social-first creative agency with offices spanning multiple continents and a client roster that includes some of the world’s most recognizable brands, among them Adidas, Amazon Music, and Lucozade. The agency creates social content and campaigns at scale, operating at the intersection of culture, creativity, and data. With just over 1,000 employees globally, We Are Social hires across an exceptionally wide range of disciplines, from content creators and community managers to global heads of research and insights.
