Many organizations are hiring to achieve SMART (specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound) goals. It has brought changes in the traditional hiring metrics. Several American organizations prefer skill-based hiring for specific tasks that require specialization, reflected in the rising number of contingent workers in the corporate ecosystem.
