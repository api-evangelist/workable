To other generations, Gen Z can be a bit of a mystery. They might expect Gen Z to be vastly different from their generation, anti-social or hyper-online due to growing up with the internet and experiencing our formative years during the COVID-19 pandemic. I can safely say that in my experience, bosses are making many assumptions about our generation based on age. While no generation can be neatly categorized without exceptions, this article serves as a launching pad to challenge your perspective on the youngest cohort of professionals.
